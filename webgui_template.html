Live planar lap simulator

3-DOF plant · lookahead driver · runs in your browser

The whole vehicle model — brush tyres, load transfer, power/grip limits — and the preview driver, ported from the Modelica plant to JavaScript. Drag a slider and the lap re-solves live (a 5000-step lap in a few milliseconds). The racing line is fixed; you are changing the car. Orange dashed = fastest real logged lap. Proof of concept: Ring Knutstorp.
